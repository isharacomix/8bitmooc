What we collect, and why we collect it

Here at #8bitmooc, we take your privacy seriously. So here is a legalese-free explanation of what we collect and why we collect it.

#8bitmooc is a website designed to teach you about technology. It's also kind of an experiment that we use to learn about how people learn programming. So we are both learning from one another.

All we collect is how you use our site: what pages you visit, how often you visit them, what times you visit them, and the code you submit for testing and grading. We also store the chat room logs (which are public anyway). We look at IP addresses to aggregate We do all of this in order to learn about how effective our resources are at teaching you, and to improve this educational experience for everyone.

We don't ask for any personal information - not even your real name. We don't use tracking cookies, and we don't do any advertising - targeted or otherwise. This is a not-for-profit endeavour, interested solely in improving technology education.

If you would like to know more about what we do, why we do it, or would like to see the information we've collected about you, please contact admin@8bitmooc.org, and we'll be happy to share it. This is an open online course, and as such, transparency is our goal.
