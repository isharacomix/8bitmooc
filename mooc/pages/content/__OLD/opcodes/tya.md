TYA (Transfer Y register to Accumulator)
========================================
Affects Flags: [[S|sign]] [[Z|zero]]

| Addressing Mode  | Usage           | Hex Code | Bytes |Cycles  |
|------------------|-----------------|---------:|------:|-------:|
| Implied          |```TYA```        | $9A      | 1     | 2      |

TYA sets the [[accumulator]] equal to the value of the
[[Y register|index_registers]].
