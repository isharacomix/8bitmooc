TAY (Transfer Accumulator to Y register)
========================================
Affects Flags: [[S|sign]] [[Z|zero]]

| Addressing Mode  | Usage           | Hex Code | Bytes |Cycles  |
|------------------|-----------------|---------:|------:|-------:|
| Implied          |```TAY```        | $A8      | 1     | 2      |

TAY sets the [[Y register|index_registers]] equal to the value of the
[[accumulator]].
