Levelling Up
============

In #8bitmooc, instead of grades, your progress is measured by an experience
point system. You can earn experience points by completing programming
challenges, breaking records, publishing games, and so on.


Growth Chart
------------
 * Level 2: 200 XP
 * Level 3: 400 XP
 * Level 4: 800 XP
 * Level 5: 1600 XP
 * Level 6: 3200 XP
 * Level 7: 6400 XP
 * Level 8: 12800 XP
