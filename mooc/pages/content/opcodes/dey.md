DEY (DEcrement Y register)
==========================
Affects Flags: [[S|sign]] [[Z|zero]]

| Addressing Mode  | Usage           | Hex Code | Bytes |Cycles  |
|------------------|-----------------|---------:|------:|-------:|
| Implied          |```DEY```        | $88      | 1     | 2      |

Reduces the [[Y register|index_registers]] by 1.
