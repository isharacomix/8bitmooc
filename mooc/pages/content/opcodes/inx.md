INX (INcrement X register)
==========================
Affects Flags: [[S|sign]] [[Z|zero]]

| Addressing Mode  | Usage           | Hex Code | Bytes |Cycles  |
|------------------|-----------------|---------:|------:|-------:|
| Implied          |```INX```        | $E8      | 1     | 2      |

Increases the [[X register|index_registers]] by 1.
