CLV (CLear oVerflow)
====================
Affects Flags: [[V|overlflow]]

| Addressing Mode  | Usage           | Hex Code | Bytes |Cycles  |
|------------------|-----------------|---------:|------:|-------:|
| Implied          |```CLV```        | $B8      | 1     | 2      |

CLV clears the [[overflow]] flag regardless of its current value. There is no
instruction for setting the overflow flag explicitly.
